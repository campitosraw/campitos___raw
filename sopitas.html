ABOUT THE PROJECT

SOPITAS is a digital platform designed to reconnect users with the essence of home cooking through a minimalist, warm, and efficient experience. In a context where 54% of consumers seek refuge in flavors that evoke the comfort of home, this project was born to transform the act of dining into a moment of well-being and sensory tranquility.

The visual identity is built upon an organic palette—deep greens and warm oranges—that reinforces the freshness of handcrafted products, while the interface prioritizes functional simplicity. Through intuitive micro-interactions and a reservation process optimized to be completed in fewer than three steps, SOPITAS strips away digital noise to focus on what truly matters: the emotional narrative of a freshly served soup and a seamless connection with local traditions.

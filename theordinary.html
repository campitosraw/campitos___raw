ABOUT THE PROJECT

This photographic project for The Ordinary explores contrast as a visual and conceptual language. The brand is built on clinical minimalism, transparency, and functional beauty — principles that guided the creative direction of the series.

The images juxtapose sterile white backgrounds with intense blue gloves, creating a visual tension between purity and intervention, minimalism and impact. The clean, clinical setting highlights the product’s transparency, while the bold chromatic contrast reinforces the brand’s scientific and uncompromising identity. Through simplicity and opposition, the series frames skincare not as luxury, but as precision.

ABOUT THE PROJECT

This illustrated campaign for Starbucks captures the vibrant, cosmopolitan energy of New York City. Designed for a modern, pop-culture-savvy audience between 20 and 40 years old , the project consists of three seasonal visual narratives: Christmas, Valentine's Day, and Summer.

The art direction relies on a flat, colorful illustrative style with soft, rounded shapes to convey warmth and approachability. By featuring diverse characters—including both customers and Starbucks employees—the illustrations celebrate the multiculturalism and inclusivity of the city while generating positive, empathetic emotions. The artwork was seamlessly adapted across multiple touchpoints, including vertical street mupis, Instagram Stories, and a custom pattern for the iconic Starbucks cups.

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Bambar is a disruptive and modern natural wine brand created to rescue almost forgotten grape varieties from the Valencian Community. Designed for a young, urban audience, it positions itself as a fresh, uncomplicated alternative to traditional wines, perfect for casual social gatherings.

The name stems from a Valencian verb meaning "to wander aimlessly," inspiring a brand philosophy that doesn't follow a map, but rather the moment. Through a bold, vibrant color palette, custom illustrations depicting intertwined walking paths, and the easygoing copy "Sin prisa ni rumbo, brinda en tu mundo," Bambar invites consumers to let go, enjoy the stroll, and celebrate the present.

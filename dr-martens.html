ABOUT THE PROJECT

This rebranding project for Dr. Martens redefines the visual identity of the brand while preserving its cultural legacy and rebellious DNA. The proposal builds a comprehensive identity system designed to reinforce authenticity, resilience, and individuality — the core values that have shaped the brand across generations.

The new visual language strengthens the iconic elements of Dr. Martens, refining its logo construction, expanding its responsive variations, and consolidating a bold black-and-yellow color system that enhances recognition and impact. The introduction of the yellow blur as a dynamic graphic resource amplifies the brand’s disruptive energy, creating a flexible yet cohesive identity adaptable to both digital and physical environments.

ABOUT THE PROJECT

TRAMA is a fashion editorial magazine that works as a reflective travel guide. Each issue invites readers to discover a city through its patterns, textures, and styles that have evolved over time.

TRAMA reveals how the past and urban memory filter into fashion, materials, workshops, markets, and the designers who reinterpret tradition. In this way, every city becomes a visual and cultural narrative; a fabric in which history, architecture, customs, and creativity are interwoven.

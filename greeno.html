ABOUT THE PROJECT

GreenO. is a sustainability-driven mobile app designed to transform recycling into a rewarding daily habit. Created for young adults navigating financial independence, the project connects environmental responsibility with tangible economic benefits. The app encourages users to recycle through a points-based system that converts sustainable actions into supermarket discounts. By linking ecological impact with immediate rewards, GreenO. bridges the gap between environmental awareness and real behavioral change. Beyond functionality, GreenO. builds a community. Through shared groups, collective goals, and a gamified experience, recycling becomes social, measurable, and motivating. The interface balances clarity and vibrancy, reflecting a generation that values both practicality and purpose.

GreenO. reframes sustainability not as an obligation, but as a lifestyle — where every scanned product becomes part of a larger environmental commitment.
